**Entertainment Games Soft (EGS)** is a private, non-typical gambling game development studio. We believe the future of the industry lies not in the "house edge," but in **technological transparency** and **the aesthetics of pure probability.**

* **Full-Stack Open Source**: From RNG (Random Number Generators) to backend engines, all our code is public on GitHub.

## üß™ Research Focus

### 1. Discrete Probability & Fair Algorithms
We are committed to achieving true **Provably Fair** systems.
Using the $\text{HMAC-SHA256}$ algorithm, we ensure that the outcome of every round is determined before it starts and remains tamper-proof.

$$P(G) = \frac{1}{\sum_{i=1}^{n} w_i}$$

### 2. Large Language Models (LLM) & Psychology
We are researching how to leverage LLMs for:
* **Dynamic Narrative Generation**: Adjusting the visual storyline of slots in real-time based on the player's betting style.
* **Responsible Gambling Alerts**: AI-driven semantic warnings triggered automatically when irrational "loss-chasing" behavior is detected.
